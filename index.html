What are the Special Features of draglet’s Exchange Software?



Liquidity Options

Market Maker

he Market Maker creates internal exchange liquidity. He steps in with predefined orders when there is no liquidity on the market to add to the orderbook. This means that if a trader wants to place a Market Buy Order and there is no one who wants to sell at the price, the Market Maker steps in as a counterparty with a spread premium.

API to External Exchange

API connection with an external provider (e.g. Kraken, Bitstamp, itBit, BitMarket) is the second way to draw liquidity. In this case external orders are drawn to the local order book. The exchange operator creates a business account at the chosen external exchange and liquidity is based on the amount deposited in the external account.



Integrated Wallet

Our exchange software includes multi-currency wallets that allow you to easily store and transact in different cryptocurrencies. Multisignature cold storage can secure all cryptocurrency funds on draglet-based exchanges, allowing you to keep your crypto funds locked up and prevent any external access.
